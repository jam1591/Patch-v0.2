WELCOME TO PATCH

This preview offers a glimpse of the game, showcasing its gameplay, features, and challenges.

You must guide Patch through a terrain teeming with relentless monsters. Each encounter demands quick thinking, precise aim, and strategic planning as you battle to outlast the ever-encroaching threats. Whether you prefer the precision of long-range firearms or a fiery intensity of an incindiary weapons, your choices will shape your adventure as you fight for survival.

ATTACK: LEFT-MOUSE-CLICK

CHANGE WEAPON: RIGHT-MOUSE-CLICK

MOVE: WASD

SKILL: TELEPORT (SPACE), HEAL (F), RUN (E)

INTERACT WITH NPC: R
